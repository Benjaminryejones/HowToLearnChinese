How to Learn Chinese
A collection of reviews, advice, and stories related to studying Mandarin

Hello, and welcome! My name is Rye. If you're interested in learning Chinese, you've come to the right place. After eight years of studying Chinese, I decided to compile lessons, tips, and more to help others improve their Mandarin language skills.

Getting Started

For those visiting the site for the first time, I'd recommend check out the links below to articles and other content related to some of the major categories related to studying Chinese. These include study technique recommendations appropriate to your proficiency level, study material reviews and recommendations, and a number of personal anecdotes about my experiences learning and living in China.

Resources Appropriate for You

Find content relevant to you by taking a look at articles filtered by proficency level.

Beginner

For those just starting out, or with up to about a year of study experience. This section also includes a number of articles on how to approach learning Chinese and work on fundamentals that students farther along might also find useful.

Intermediate

Appropriate for students who have been studying for several years but do not yet have the complete combination of solid listening, speaking, reading, and writing skills required for basic fluency.

Advanced

Advanced students are probably considered "fluent" by non-Chinese speakers, and can understand a large amount of conversation topics, media, and even specialized content--at least, with the occasional help of a dictionary.
